ABOUT US

When you work with us, you are part of a team of comitted collaborators. All aspects of our software development process are intergrated, interactive

and agile.

Our approach unifies design, development and product management to create exceptional products.

We start each project by understanding your business goals, the user's needs and the product requirements.

Then we help translate them into effective and elegant solutions.

Jkuat Studios Ltd. is a company registered in Kenya, with a vision to improve the quality of digital content. We are passionately African, and passionate to create content that positively reflects the stories of Africa. Located in the suburb of Juja, Jkuat Studios Ltd. offers a spacious facility for recordings and rehearsals. The studio is fully equipped with professional gear for audio production and music. This is a one-stop shop for all your audio production needs. Our staff are dedicated to offer exemplary services. We maintain talent that is full of experience and up-to-date with current trends in the audio production realm. Additionally, the facility may be hired out, should a client wish to use an engineer of their choice.
